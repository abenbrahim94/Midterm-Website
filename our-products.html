Our Products

GROLSCH PREMIUM PILSNER

We’ve been brewing beers of real substance since we started in Grolle in 1615. Our Premium Pilsner has a naturally green hop aroma, a crisp finish and a clean, confident bitterness from the combination of two Hallertau hops: Emerald and Magnum. This is joined by supporting malty flavours from both pale and specifically chosen malts.

The result is a pilsner with depth of taste, that naturally appeals to a more developed palette.

GROLSCH RADLER

The Grolsch Radler delivers real substance with their super refreshing taste, rooted in real beer combined with real fruit juice. Both the 2% and 0.0% are naturally brewed from carefully selected ingredients mixed with real fruit and sparkling water from our spring

The result is a balance of lemon acidity, malty sweetness and bitterness that makes them both super refreshing and thirst quenching.

Grolsch Radler is made by our master brewers real lemon juice. This adds a freshness and quality to the taste that makes our Radler the perfect, natural refreshment.

Available in Canada, France, the Netherlands and Spain.

GROLSCH WEIZEN

Grolsch Puur Weizen delivers its substance from the mix of wheat and barley malts, original German Weizen yeast and the Magnum bitter hop – which together give us the unique and full-flavoured refreshment of our Weizen.

Our Weizen is a golden color wheat beer - refreshing with sparkling, spicy aromas of cloves and ripe fruits.

Its flavours combine wonderfully into a refreshing and light taste that is different from the traditional, more heavy, German Weizen beers. This is a key reason why our beer was crowned world’s best Weizen the first year it was created, by the World Beer Association in 2007.

Available in Canada, Italy, Japan, France, the Netherlands, Romania, South Korea, and Switzerland.

GROLSCH 0.0%

Drawing on more than 400 years of brewing progression, we use a special yeast (and advanced brewing techniques) to craft our natural 0,0% alcohol beer. The unique full fermentation process creates 0,0% alcohol without compromise on our full flavour.

Grolsch 0,0% has a fresh, hoppy and full bodied taste with a pleasant, crisp after taste – that makes it the perfect thirst quencher for every moment of the day.

To get it just right, we conducted many brewing trials and spent hours developing a full flavour. To do this, we use a special yeast that delivers all the aroma but produces no alcohol. It took time, but it was worth it – because in 2018 we received the Gold Medal for non-alcoholic beer at the European Beer Star competition.

Last year, the World Beer Awards voted Grolsch 0.0% as ‘Netherland’s Best Light Beer’, and now there’s more.

The European Beer Star Awards also selected our 0.0% as their best bottom fermented non-alcoholic beer (technical term for the production of beers like lager or pilsner) and awarded us with gold for our genuine, high-quality and full character beer.

Winning these awards is an honour we couldn’t have achieved without the passion and hard work of our people.

Available in Canada, the Netherlands and South Korea.
